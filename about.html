About Me

Welcome to my digital space! I'm Manaswi Patil, a computer science enthusiast currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science & Engineering at Prof. Ram Meghe Institute of Technology and Research in Badnera, India. I've maintained a commendable CGPA of 9.2/10.0, overcoming geographical challenges in Amravati, Maharashtra, to fuel my passion for computer science.

My journey in this field began with childhood curiosity, watching my mom work on her office laptop. This interest blossomed into a deep love for computers and technology, leading me to pursue a Bachelor's degree filled with diverse projects and a focus on artificial intelligence and machine learning. During internships at Corizo and Pantech Solutions, I contributed to chatbot remodeling and developed a predictive maintenance algorithm, showcasing my practical skills.

Beyond academics, I've organized a large-scale gaming LAN tournament, been a vital part of the college women's chess team, and led the computer science division of the go-kart team. My research endeavors, including the exploration of Bayesian Neural Networks published in the International Journal for Research in Applied Science & Engineering Technology, reflect my commitment to pushing boundaries.

As I look ahead to graduate studies in both computer science and machine learning, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise, research acumen, and a passion for continuous learning to the table.
